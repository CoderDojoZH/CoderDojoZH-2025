Material

The materials created by us come in two flavours:

  • a PDF ready to display on the screen, or print out page after page.
  • a booklet ready for printing instructions - the same as we use during our workshops

In the spirit of OpenSource we also share our learning materials.

You can find all of them in our GitHub repository

Lightbot

Lightbot is a simple, but not always easy game helping to learn about base constructs like conditions, loops, procedures.

Scratch

A very popular, widely used (also in schools), and open tool for programming. It is well a thought and designed programming environment. It enables making of many different games, shows, quizzes. There are millions of programs created in Scratch. Don't let the colorful page deceive you!

Remember to register first, to store and keep expanding your projects.

  • booklets with step-by-step instructions to build specific game
